Felícia Fuster web

Diseño de una web para la celebración de los 100 años de la poeta y artista catalana Felícia Fuster. Es una web que sirve para dar a conocer la vida y obra de esta autora, en la qual se ha intentado acercar a un público joven.

This is a design of a website in celebration of the 100 years of the Catalan poet and artist Felícia Fuster. It is a website that serves to publicize the life and work of this author in which she has tried to approach a young audience.
